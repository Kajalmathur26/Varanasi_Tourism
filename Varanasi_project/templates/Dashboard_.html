Varanasi, or Benaras, also known as Kashi is one of the oldest living cities in the world. Varanasis Prominence in Hindu mythology is virtually unrevealed. Benaras is older than history, older than tradition, older even than legend and looks twice as old as all of them put together. The land of Varanasi Kashi has been the ultimate pilgrimage spot for Hindus for ages. Ganges in Varanasi is believed to have the power to wash away the sins of mortals. Ganges is said to have its origins in the tresses of Lord Shiva and in Varanasi, it expands to the mighty river that we know of. The city is a center of learning and civilization for over 3000 years.

MAHASHIVRATRI FESTIVAL IN VARANASI

Maha Shivaratri (during February-March), dedicated to Lord Shiva, is the most important religious festival in Varanasi. Festivities are held throughout the city.

HOLI

Varanasi is a beautiful place Holi is actually one of the most important Varanasi festivals. It is celebrated with a lot of pomp and show.

Ganga Mahotsav

Ganga Mahotsav is one of the most popular Varanasi festivals. This is believed holy river Ganga provides nourishment and life to the entire civilization
